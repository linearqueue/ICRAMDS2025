About Manit

MANIT Bhopal

Maulana Azad National Institute of Technology (MANIT) Bhopal is a prestigious Institution of National Importance located in the heart of India. Formerly known as Maulana Azad College of Technology (MACT), MANIT has a rich history of providing quality technical education and contributing to the development of the nation.

History and Legacy

Established in 1960, MANIT was jointly sponsored by the Government of India and the Government of Madhya Pradesh. The institute was named after the esteemed scholar and the first Education Minister of independent India, Maulana Abul Kalam Azad. Since its inception, MANIT has been dedicated to producing highly skilled, talented, and qualified manpower in various fields of engineering and technology.

Academic Programs

MANIT offers a wide range of academic programs to nurture the next generation of innovators and leaders. The institute provides undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs across diverse disciplines. These programs are designed to impart in-depth knowledge, foster critical thinking, and promote research and development.

Commitment to Excellence

MANIT is committed to excellence in teaching, research, and innovation. The institute strives to provide a conducive learning environment that encourages intellectual curiosity, creativity, and a passion for lifelong learning. With its focus on quality education and holistic development, MANIT continues to be a leading institution in the field of technical education.
