How to Reach MANIT Bhopal?

Arrival by Road

Bhopal is also connected by a good network of national and state highways. You can reach Bhopal by bus or car from neighboring cities and states. Once you arrive in Bhopal, you can use local transport to reach the MANIT campus.

Arrival by Air

Bhopal has its own airport, Raja Bhoj International Airport (BHO), which connects it to major cities in India. Upon arrival at the airport, you can hire a taxi or use app-based cab services to reach MANIT.

Arrival by Train

Bhopal Junction (BPL) is the main railway station and is well-connected to the extensive Indian rail network. You can easily find trains from major cities across India to Bhopal. From the railway station, taxis and auto-rickshaws are readily available to take you to MANIT.
