Registration & Payment

Registration & Payment Process

  • One of the authors of the accepted paper must register and present the paper for inclusion in the conference proceedings.
  • The participant must complete the registration and payment process via the registration link after receiving a paper acknowledgement.
  • The conference registration fee includes a kit, admission to all technical sessions, lunch, tea/coffee, and snacks.
  • Do not forget to record transaction details and a screenshot of the transaction, which will be required when registering.
