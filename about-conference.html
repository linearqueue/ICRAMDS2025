About Conference

ICRAMDS - 2025

The International Conference on Recent Advances in Mathematics and Data Science (ICRAMDS-2025) is a premier forum for the exchange of cutting-edge research and ideas in the fields of mathematics and data science.

This conference aims to bring together faculty, scientists, researchers, and students from both industry and academia to share their latest findings and advancements. ICRAMDS-2025 will be held in hybrid mode, offering both virtual and in-person participation options, ensuring accessibility for a global audience.

Conference Objectives

ICRAMDS-2025 has the following key objectives:

  • To provide a platform for student, researchers and dissemination of the latest research in mathematics and data science.
  • To foster collaboration and networking among researchers, scientists, and industry professionals.
  • To promote innovation and advancements in the theoretical and practical applications of mathematics and data science.
  • To facilitate discussions on emerging trends and challenges in these dynamic fields.
  • To offer opportunities for professional development through keynote presentations, invited lectures, and papers.

Conference Highlights

ICRAMDS-2025 will feature a diverse range of activities, including:

  • Keynote lectures by renowned experts in mathematics and data science.
  • Invited talks by leading researchers.
  • Paper presentations showcasing original research contributions.
  • Certificates for participants presenting papers and attending the program.

Themes of ICRAMDS - 2025

  • Algebra, Linear Algebra and Geometry
  • Number Theory and Numerical Analysis
  • Mathematical Modeling and Fluid Dynamics
  • Blockchain Security and Applications
  • Financial Mathematics
  • Quantum Computing
  • Cyber security and Privacy
  • Software Architecture and Design Pattern
  • Artificial Intelligence
  • Machine Learning and Deep Learning
  • Next Generation Network
  • Discrete Mathematics and Graph Theory
  • Fixed Point Theory and Fuzzy Mathematics
  • Cryptography and Coding Theory
  • Operations Research
  • Probability and Statistics
  • High-Performance Computing
  • Cloud Computing
  • Internet of Things
  • Information Retrieval
  • Data warehousing and Mining
  • Computer Vision and Image Processing

Publication Opportunities

Selected papers presented at ICRAMDS-2025 will be submitted to Springer for possible publication in Springer Proceedings after a standard peer-review process.

Publication in Springer Proceedings is subject to the final decision of Springer.
