About the Conference

The Department of Mathematics, Bioinformatics, and Computer Applications is proud to announce the International Conference on Mathematics and Data Science (ICRAMDS-2025), which will be organized in a hybrid mode â€” offering both virtual and in-person participation options. The conference proceedings will be published in Springer - CCIS series (indexed in Scopus, SCImago, DBLP, Google Scholar). This conference aims to provide a dynamic platform for students, scientists, researchers, and professionals from industry and academia to come together and share their latest research findings, innovative ideas, and technological advancements in the diverse fields of Mathematics and Data Science. By bringing together a wide spectrum of participants, ICRAMDS-2025 seeks to foster interdisciplinary collaborations, inspire new research directions, and strengthen the exchange of knowledge across these critical domains.

The conference program will feature a rich blend of activities, including keynote addresses by eminent speakers, invited talks by distinguished researchers, and paper presentation sessions where participants can showcase their original work. This interactive environment will facilitate meaningful discussions, networking opportunities, and professional development. Certificates will be awarded to all registered participants who present their papers and attend the sessions, recognizing their valuable contributions and engagement with the conference. ICRAMDS-2025 promises to be an enriching academic event that not only highlights cutting-edge research but also encourages the growth and interaction of the global Mathematics and Data Science community.
