About Department

About Department

The Department of Mathematics, Bioinformatics & Computer Applications at MANIT is a dynamic and thriving academic centre committed to pushing the boundaries of knowledge in mathematical sciences and their diverse applications.

Our Focus

The department emphasizes a strong tradition of excellence in teaching, research, and innovation. We are proud of our distinguished faculty, comprising accomplished academicians and researchers with expertise in a wide range of areas, including:

  • Pure and Applied Mathematics
  • Bioinformatics
  • Computer Science

Research and Innovation

The department is actively involved in cutting-edge research, with dedicated research groups exploring exciting and relevant fields such as:

  • Cryptography
  • Blockchains
  • Mathematical Modeling.
  • Machine Learning
  • Bioinformatics
  • Data Science

To support this research, students have access to state-of-the-art computational labs and software tools.

Our Commitment

We are committed to providing an innovative and industry-relevant curriculum that prepares our graduates to excel in an increasingly complex world. Our collaborative initiatives and global partnerships further enhance the educational experience and ensure that our students are exceptionally well-prepared to meet emerging challenges in science, technology, and society.
